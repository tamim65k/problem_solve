In this game, every character has a common attribute — health points, shortened to HP.</p><p>In general, different values of HP are grouped into $$$4$$$ categories:</p><ul> <li> Category $$$A$$$ if HP is in the form of $$$(4 n + 1)$$$, that is, when divided by $$$4$$$, the remainder is $$$1$$$; </li><li> Category $$$B$$$ if HP is in the form of $$$(4 n + 3)$$$, that is, when divided by $$$4$$$, the remainder is $$$3$$$; </li><li> Category $$$C$$$ if HP is in the form of $$$(4 n + 2)$$$, that is, when divided by $$$4$$$, the remainder is $$$2$$$; </li><li> Category $$$D$$$ if HP is in the form of $$$4 n$$$, that is, when divided by $$$4$$$, the remainder is $$$0$$$. </li></ul><p>The above-mentioned $$$n$$$ can be any integer.</p><p>These $$$4$$$ categories ordered from highest to lowest as $$$A  \gt  B  \gt  C  \gt  D$$$, which means category $$$A$$$ is the highest and category $$$D$$$ is the lowest.</p><p>While playing the game, players can increase the HP of the character. Now, Tokitsukaze wants you to increase her HP by at most $$$2$$$ (that is, either by $$$0$$$, $$$1$$$ or $$$2$$$).
